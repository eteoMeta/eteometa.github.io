About MetaPass IDDefi lending is incredibly capital inefficient with average collateralization of 150%. Credit assessments can only be done at the wallet level resulting in mostly thin-file borrowers. At MetaPass ID, we aim to solve both problems by issuing a unique and interoperable Verified Credential, tying to a credit rating aggregated across chains and wallets. Contact us for more information on how we are building the credit bureau for Web3.
